Smart Features

School Management

The core usage of this feature is to manage daily classrooms activities, quickly update school details and photos without missing anything overtime, manage school bank details for fee collection, keep track of student promotions, and academic year start wizard.

Student Management

This feature provides a complete overview of the student's data including academics, extracurricular activities, fee dues, leaves, etc. It is helpful in many aspects like spending any important reminders, updating student details, report card presentations, ID card generation, TC management and student promotion.

Leave Management

This feature has many uses that include approval of leave applications for staff, and holiday calendar available from the start of the year till the end.

Attendance Management

Approval of leave applications for staff and students where QR code scan is exclusively available for school staff, automatically marking leaves when attendance is being missed, generating attendance reports everyday, notifications being sent to parents if the child is absent at school.

Fee Management

The fee management feature helps in fee planning, report handling, providing reminders of due dates, concession, receipt issuing and fine-fee management for late fee payments without any raised concerns.

Transportation Management

This feature provides accurate vehicle travel reports, manages routes, live tracking, pop-up notification on the bus leaving timings, student status update, and finds optimal routes for pickup.
